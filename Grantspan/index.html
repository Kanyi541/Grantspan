We help you see the world differently and uncover opportunities you may have never imagined, while achieving results that bridge current realities with future possibilities. Our mandate is to provide our users with up-to-date, relevant, and comprehensive information on funding and capacity development opportunities. We diligently work to keep our subscribers informed about every opportunity that can foster their growth—whether through grant calls, donations, prizes, or any other beneficial resource for African-based organizations. We feature a wide range of opportunities in numerous fundable areas, including governance, health, development, and social inclusion, alongside showcasing grant-makers from around the world. Globally, there are thousands of philanthropists, charitable foundations, and government entities providing funds through grants, donations, and gifts to support entities and individuals committed to the betterment of humanity. Grantspan’s role is to bridge this gap and ensure that these opportunities connect with those who need them most.
